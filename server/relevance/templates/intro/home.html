The iPhone application that tracks the news that impacts your friends

Snocone finds news that impact your friends, notifying you about news anytime there's an important update, as well as finding you articles that your friends care about, so you can have a richer conversation and more meaningful connection with your friends

App Features: allowing you to stay connected with your friends and family!

Friend Feed Personalized article feeds

Find the latest headlines that impact your friends, enabling you to have a richer discussion with your friends when you see them!

Share Articles Harness social media

You can choose to text your friends, or share articles on your Facebook or Twitter!

Save Favorites Keep your bookmarks

Save your favorite articles to read for later, or to contact the friends it impacts!

Headlines that impact your friends

Say there's an earthquake in San Francisco over the summer. Snocone will help you stay connected by notifying you that there's an important update, and also suggest that you reach out to your friends who are in the area over the summer. These news can also be found on Snocone's iPhone app, featured in the main screen that displays as soon as the application is launched.

News that relate to your friends' interests

Meeting with a coworker, casual acquaintance or business connection for a lunch? Simply launch Snocone's iPhone application, and type in the name of the person you're meeting. We'll pull up a list of articles in the news that's relevant to the person's likes, allowing you to have a richer conversation.
